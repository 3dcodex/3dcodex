## 🧠 About Me

I'm a **Software Engineering** graduate from **ICT University, Yaoundé**, passionate about building secure, scalable, and innovative digital solutions.

I'm the **Co-founder of VectorSpace**, a platform designed to connect creators, developers, and innovators into one powerful ecosystem for collaboration, growth, and opportunity.

I also created **Unity Mentorship Hub (UMH)** — a platform focused on guiding and mentoring aspiring developers in their learning journey.
